About company

Once upon a time one of our team mate Dr. Sonia, M.D, was posted in rural health center, Mangliya. She was the incharge of the center. Being incharge she needs to adress all type of patients. Mostly in that area people suffered with cosmetic problems due to insufficient hygiene. Acne, pimples, dandruff, fungal and various ailments regarding skin. She was helpless due to lack of specialist, and also Pt. Can't afford high fees of Dermatologist due to which the situation became worst for that area. So without wasting time she did Fellowship in the field of Cosmetology to help her patients. With Patience's and timely management of various type of cosmetic related problems of patients, she conquered her lagging field in no tym. Today she is the leading practitioner in Our setup, with clinical experience of almost 6 yrs. Today , with this success story we had treated various patients in the pocket fare. We feel proud to say our tagline- " Cosmetic care within Pocket fare." Team Snehcare.
